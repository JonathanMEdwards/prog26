We thank the reviewers for their effort and comments. The chair identified the need for four major revisions:

1. Add evidence beyond single examples for the 4 problems addressed by the work.
2. Add a systematic discussion of what is necessary to support further operations, and what the limitations are.
3. Provide a deeper explanation of the conclusions from previous study.
4. Provide more details of the evaluation with a discussion.

Accordingly, we have expanded and restructured the evaluation aspects of the paper. Before there was an Evaluation section that tabulated the eight challenge problems identified in our prior paper (Schema Evolution in Interactive Programming Systems), assigning a Pass/Fail grade to each. Now we have distributed discussion of case studies throughout the paper and provide a more nuanced evaluation. These include the four challenge problems that were deemed to have been solved, but also other cases taken from the research literature that reveal more comprehensively the current capabilities and limitations of Baseline.
